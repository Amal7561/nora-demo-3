What is Nora.Legal

Nora.Lega’s vision is to empower solo attorneys and small law firms with tactical solutions, enabling them to compete with large law on a level playing field. We are committed to providing our clients with simplified and contextual legal solutions, leveraging expertise, efficient processes and customized technology at the right price.

Nora.Lega makes attorneys’ lives easier with its innovative online legal marketplace that is open twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week and its bespoke legal research and writing solutions, including Contract Review, Bankruptcy Document Collection, Receivership Service, Research Memos, Motion and Appellate Briefs, Pre-Trial Support, Transactional Support, Discovery services and more covering all aspects of legal support.

Uniquely tailored to your needs, our tech-enabled legal solutions help you meet strict deadlines and manage fluctuating workloads on economical fixed-fee basis.
